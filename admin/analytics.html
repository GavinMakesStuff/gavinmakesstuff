Setup guide

Follow these steps once to get analytics tracking on your site.

1

Create a Google Analytics account

Go to analytics.google.com, sign in with your Google account, and click "Start measuring." Choose a property name like "Gavin Makes Stuff," set your timezone and currency, then create a Web data stream for gavinmakesstuff.com.

2

Copy your Measurement ID

In GA4 → Admin → Data Streams → click your stream → copy the Measurement ID (starts with G-). Paste it into the field above and click Save & Apply.

3

Add the tracking script to your site pages

Once saved, copy the code block below and add it to the <head> of every HTML page (index.html, studio/index.html, portfolio/index.html, contact.html, studio/project.html, etc.). There's a commented-out placeholder already in index.html — just uncomment it and swap in your real ID.

Enter your Measurement ID above to see the code snippet.
4

Verify data is coming in

After adding the script, visit your site, then check GA4 → Reports → Realtime. You should see yourself appear. It can take 24–48 hours for full historical data to populate. Enable "Exclude my own visits" above first so your setup visits don't skew the data.
